Things To Reduce Air Pollution

By:Parik Mehta

The problem of air pollution is getting very serious, so it is our turn to be
serious. Here are some things you can do in order to by yourself, at home, in
order to stop air pollution.

Conserve Energy

Conserving energy is a great way to lower your amount of pollutants and pollution
released in the air. This helps to reduce air pollution as they are decreasing the
amount of pollution released on a daily basis. This is one of the most common way to
conserve energy, and save our environment. Here are some things you can do to conserve
energy: use less energy at home, office, work, etc, use alternative sources of energy
that are renewable, and basically use less amount of energy to get the same job done.

Transportation

Using transportation is one important part of our society today, it is the way we get to
other places quickly, and fastly, but it has a side-effect. Motor powered transportation uses
fossil fuels that releases pollutants that are a danger to our air and ozone layer. If we
lessen the use of motor powered vehicles, then we can decrease the amount of pollutants released.
Here are some ways to use less motor powered vehicles: Carpool (using less cars to get to the
same place), use public transportation, bicycle to your destination, walk, and stakeboarding.

Recycling and Reusing

Recycling is a great way to reduce air pollution, as it reduces the amount of methane sent to
the air, and methane is harmful to our atmosphere. Recycling and reusing can also help save
resources, like paper, cloth, wood, etc. Things you can recycle or reuse include: plastic, paper,
metal, wood, fabric.

Plantation

Plantation helps us have more trees, which would reduce the amount of CO2 in the air. It would also
help our world, by giving more oxygen, in addition to making a habitat for animals. Planting a tree
in open areas helps in plantation. Also, encourage your family, and friends to do the same.
